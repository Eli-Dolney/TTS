eli-neutral: Lists.
eli-neutral: Think of a list as a row of boxes that can hold anything.
eli-neutral: Lists are ordered and mutable; you can add, remove, and modify items easily.
eli-neutral: Use lists when order matters.

eli-neutral: Tuples.
eli-neutral: Tuples are like lists that cannot be changed after creation.
eli-neutral: They are great for fixed values like coordinates.
eli-neutral: Because they are immutable, tuples can be used as dictionary keys.

eli-neutral: Sets.
eli-neutral: Sets store unique items without guaranteed order.
eli-neutral: They excel at membership tests and math style operations: union, intersection, and difference.

eli-neutral: Dictionaries.
eli-neutral: Dictionaries store key value pairs.
eli-neutral: Think lookup: provide a key to get a value.
eli-neutral: Perfect for modeling structured data like a student record or product catalog.

eli-neutral: Nesting collections.
eli-neutral: You can nest these structures for real world data.
eli-neutral: A list of dictionaries is common in apps; each dictionary represents an object, like a contact or a user.

eli-neutral: Project: contact book.
eli-neutral: Add contacts, search by name, list them all, and prevent duplicates.
eli-neutral: Use a list to store contact records, a set to track unique names, and dictionaries for contact details.
eli-neutral: This shows how collections work together.

eli-neutral: Recap.
eli-neutral: Lists are ordered and mutable.
eli-neutral: Tuples are ordered and immutable.
eli-neutral: Sets are unordered and unique.
eli-neutral: Dictionaries map keys to values.
eli-neutral: Nesting creates flexible, real world structures.
