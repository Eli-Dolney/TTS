eli-neutral: Many styluses connect via Bluetooth and support pressure sensitivity and shortcut buttons.
eli-neutral: Compatibility matters; not every stylus works with every device.

eli-neutral: Headsets.
eli-neutral: Headsets combine headphones and microphone for calls, meetings, and gaming.
eli-neutral: Wired headsets may use USB or TRRS, Tip Ring Ring Sleeve, the three point five millimeter connector that carries stereo and mic.
eli-neutral: Some Apple devices used Lightning for wired headsets.
eli-neutral: Wireless headsets use Bluetooth and offer mobility but require battery management.
eli-neutral: IT tip: keep a wired fallback for long days.

eli-neutral: Speakers.
eli-neutral: Built in device speakers are limited.
eli-neutral: External Bluetooth speakers provide better audio for music, video, and presentations.
eli-neutral: They are portable and battery powered.

eli-neutral: Cameras and webcams.
eli-neutral: Phones and many laptops have built in cameras.
eli-neutral: Desktops and some laptops may need a USB webcam for conferencing or recording.
eli-neutral: Most webcams are plug and play.

eli-neutral: Docking stations.
eli-neutral: Docking stations act as hubs so a single connection links monitors, keyboard, mouse, Ethernet, and storage.
eli-neutral: They add ports and sometimes support full size expansion cards.
eli-neutral: Ideal for professionals who need desktop like connectivity with a laptop.

eli-neutral: Port replicators.
eli-neutral: A lighter, more universal alternative to docks, usually connected over USB.
eli-neutral: Adds USB ports, audio, video outputs, and sometimes SD readers.
eli-neutral: Great for travel; typically no large adapter card support.

eli-neutral: Trackpads.
eli-neutral: Laptops include a built in trackpad with multi touch gestures like pinch and two finger scroll.
eli-neutral: External Bluetooth trackpads bring the same gestures to desktops.
eli-neutral: Many laptops offer a function key to disable the trackpad while typing.

eli-neutral: Drawing pads, digitizer tablets.
eli-neutral: Provide precise stylus input for artists, designers, and engineers.
eli-neutral: Connect via USB or Bluetooth and support pressure sensitivity for natural strokes.
